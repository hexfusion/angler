Shipping Times

You can see the estimated arrival dates of your order during checkout.

To calculate the estimated arrival dates, we add the time it takes to process your order before it ships plus the time it takes to arrive at your shipping address after it leaves our warehouse.

Most packages in your order can be tracked via our carriers' websites. It may take up to 1 business day for your information to be posted on the carrier's website from the date your order is shipped. Order tracking is available for orders placed within the last 60 days. Sign in to your account to track a shipped order.

We're sorry, at this time we cannot provide tracking information for international shipments.

Please note:
West Branch Angler does its best to ensure your delivery arrives on time. However, delivery dates are not guaranteed and your order may arrive after the estimated arrival date for various reasons, including unforeseen delays.

For example, your order may arrive after the estimated arrival date you see in checkout for any of the following reasons:

  • Delay in courier pickup due to holidays
  • Order contains items with longer than 2 day processing time, except books
  • Large orders that have more than 25 items may require longer processing time
  • Delivery is to a P.O. Box or APO/FPO address
  • Delivery is outside the 48 continental United States
  • Delays for Canada and International orders due to customs clearance
  • Delays due to problems with credit card authorization and verification
  • Problems with uploaded designs or media that do not meet our recommended requirements encountered during processing of your order

Shipping Methods

West Branch Angler offers several shipping methods to choose from. You will see which shipping methods are available for your shipping address during checkout.

Depending on where your order is shipped, the following shipping methods may be available:

Shipping Method Estimated Arrival

UPS Ground Usually arrives in 3 - 4 business days
USPS Priority Mail Usually arrives in 3 - 6 business days
USPS Express Mail Usually arrives in 2 - 3 business days

1 Day Shipping
Most items ordered by noon EST on a business day will arrive next business day. Orders placed after noon EST will arrive in 2 business days.

Large orders that contain more than 25 items, may require longer processing times will arrive the next business day after they are processed and shipped.

Canada & International Orders
West Branch Angler does offer shipping to Canada and other countries outside the U.S. However, you may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are levied once the package reaches your country. Additional charges for customs clearance are the responsibility of the recipient. Customs policies vary widely from country to country; please contact your local customs office for further information. Additionally, when ordering from West Branch Angler.com, you are considered the importer of record and must comply with all laws and regulations of the country in which you are receiving the goods. West Branch Angler will only ship international orders to the customers billing address with no exceptions.

Customs forms for all international packages list the value of your order's contents by product type. For instance, orders containing T-shirts, Fly Rod, and Fly Line will be listed in a way similar to the following example:

T-shirt value: $25.99
Fly Rod value: $379.99
Fly Line value: $59.99

Multiple Shipments/ Back Orders /Drop Ships

The items in your order may arrive in different shipments. Depending on the type, quantity and weight of items in your order, we may pack and ship them in different packages to ensure that they arrive on time and undamaged. Most packages are trackable via the carriers' websites. Sign into your account to track an order. Packages that are trackable will have a 'Track' button on the Order History and Order Detail page. Some items might be drop shipped or backordered due to inventory errors. Items that are backordered will be noted on the packing slip. If you would like to cancel the backorder you may contact us at anytime before it is shipped to do so.

Returns & Exchanges

West Branch Angler.com offers a 30-day money back guarantee on all products less a 15% restocking fee. If you would like to return or exchange an item, please contact us to fill out a request form, and we will send you an RMA and instructions on how to send the item(s) back. Shipping charges are not refundable.
