About The West Branch Angler

Situated on the banks of the beautiful West Branch of the Delaware River, the West Branch Angler & Sportsman's Resort is located off NY Route 17 between the villages of Deposit and Hancock New York. We are within easy access from most all of the major metropolitan cities in the northeast.

"Perhaps the best trout fishery in the East is on the dam tailwaters of the Upper Delaware River System." - Fly Fish America
We are a fly fishing resort and shooting preserve, offering 26 luxury cabins on 200 acres of exquisite mountain scenery. With 3 miles of private catch and release water on the West Branch of the Delaware, you can choose to fish the river here at the resort, go on a guided river trip with one of our pro guides, fly fish our stocked trout and bass pond, shoot sporting clays or hunt our preserve. Our log cabins range from cozy studio size all the way up to luxury personified, with the multi-bedroom CEO's and Executive cabins sporting a host of amenities. Our accommodations blend in with the area's natural surroundings, and are designed to accommodate guests from the budget minded to those who desire the very best. From 1 person to 100, you will find our hospitality warm and welcoming. Our friendly management and staff are always ready to assist you during your stay with us.

Our Mission Statement

Our passion is to provide you with premier customer service. Our goals are to create an atmosphere at our resort that allows you to relax and enjoy every aspect of your time away from work. We strive to have the ultimate product knowledge, the finest products and to share our years of expertise that allow you to experience and enjoy the outdoor sports of fly fishing, preserve shooting, wild turkey and grouse hunts, and sporting clays to the fullest. To this end, we are committed to the following:

  • The Latest in Products And Services
  • Up to Date Product Knowledge for Your Educated Buy
  • Complete Tackle Rigging and Service
  • A Trained Sales Staff Who Fish and Shoot
  • Local Fishing Information and Tips
  • Local Conservation and Environmental Support
  • Fly Casting and Shooting Schools
  • The Best Professional Guides on the River System

We are enthusiastic about our outdoor sports and are committed to sharing both our knowledge and expertise in hopes of passing along the exitement, challange and tradition of the sports we love. We believe this is what makes staying at our resort so unique.
