Sporting Clays at West Branch Angler

Sporting Clays is one of the fastest growing sports in the country. In keeping with this increased popularity our newly redesigned and expanded course has dramatically added to the diversity of our target presentations. The fun and excitement of the game is found in the many varied clay targets thrown to simulate real life shooting challenges like driven pheasants, springing teal, ruffed grouse, woodcock, quail, rabbit and others. Shooting hours are regularly scheduled. The minimum reservation is for two shooters (single shooters MUST shoot a double round). Shooting is limited to five people in one group. Groups larger than five will be split into smaller groups. You must bring your own shotgun. Our new clays course with 12 automatic stations is set amidst the hillsides among the old growth hemlocks, open hardwood forests and ancient orchards, creating scenery that has to been seen to truly appreciated. Our course was designed by John Higgins, and will challenge the experienced shooter yet will be enjoyable for the novice. The course is great fun but in an effort to make the course like real hunting it will prove to be very physically challenging, please plan accordingly. Come and try sporting clays at The West Branch Angler, and you'll agree that we have done it right!

NOTICE: If you are interested in shooting our course we do have a 24 hour reservation policy please contact the shop directly to add this to your reservation (no exceptions). All shoots will take place after 12:00 EST and guests will have precedent over non guests.
