Boat Rentals





The West Branch Angler Resort and the Wild Rainbow Outfitters in Hancock, New York, rent a variety of watercraft to fit your needs at both locations. Specifically designed for stability and maneuverability they are the ultimate riverboats for fly-fishing.Canoes are available for rent and include personal flotation devices and paddles. These are great for a day excursion to see the river scenery or fall foliage.
