Resort Policies


Deposits:
50% deposit is required at time of reservation. Deposits may be in the form of cash, money order or check. Reservations will not be held and are not confirmed until a deposit has been received. If a deposit has not been received at least 30 days prior to the date of arrival, the reservation will be automatically cancelled. Credit cards are acceptable for deposits only on reservations made 30 days prior to the date of the actual stay. Deposits of 50% on guide trips are required at time of reservation. Deposits are not transferable.

Cancellations/Refunds:
If you cancel 14 days before your scheduled day of arrival, we will refund your deposit, less 10% handling fee or apply your deposit to a future stay within the current year. Cancellations with less than 14 days notice do not qualify for a refund even if due to illness, work schedule changes or inclement weather conditions. If you cancel with less than 14 days notice, and we are able to fill your cancelled reservation, we will apply your lodging deposit, less 10% handling fee, to a future stay during the current year only.

Guides are independent contractors, therefore deposits are forwarded directly to the guide. Deposits on canceled guide trips may be applied to a future trip within the current year at the sole discretion of the guide.

Children:
Children 9 years old or younger stay free. Children under 9 will be charged $3.50 per day for breakfast.

Rollaway and Cribs:
Available upon request at no charge.

Pets:
Dogs (2 maximum) are allowed @ $10/night/pet. Pets must be kept on a leash at all times when outside the cabin, and cleaned up after. 1 hour early departure is required. Guest is responsible for any damages done by the pet. One dog used for hunting our preserve, or for a guided bird hunt, is allowed at no charge beginning October 1st of each season.

Credit Cards:
MasterCard, Visa and American Express are accepted for payment.

Minimum Stay:
2 nights minimum stay is required on weekends (Fri/Sat) for the entire season. 3 night minimum for Memorial Day, July 4th, and Labor Day.

Check-In/Out-Times:
Check in time is after 3:00pm. Room availability cannot be assured before that time. Arrival prior to official check-in time is at your own risk.

Check out time is no later than 11:00am.

Additional Persons:
$50 per person per night is added.

Taxes/gratuities:
8.25% tax New York sales tax, 3% local county occupancy tax, and gratuities are not included in any published rates.
