Group & Corporate Events

We welcome corporate outings and large groups with the same enthusiasm as we do individual guests. Our facilities are geared to enhance almost any large group's meeting needs & outdoor activities. Whether your group is from the business world, a club gathering, or for social activities, we welcome all with the same enthusiasm as with our individual guests.

 
Addition Update August 2004

For 2004 our River Run Restaurant is being increased in size with an addition of 1800 square feet. It will be devoted exclusively to meeting space that can be used as one meeting room or two.
With this expansion, the meeting and dining rooms will be completely separate giving complete privacy for your group to hold their meeting in, then dine either in the main dining room or on the spacious outdoor deck.
 
Corporate Events/Meetings
Corporate events are easily accommodated. The main restaurant, both meeting rooms and the TroutSkellar are all available if needed. The new meeting rooms were designed with today's high tech needs in mind, with satellite internet connections, and audio visual needs ready for any type of meeting. In addition, for larger groups the entire resort may be reserved at a special rate. Call us for more details for what will be a totally unique business/activity meeting for your company.
