The River Run Restaurant serving dinners, banquets, and corporate meetings can accommodate your every need. Enjoy the warm summer nights while dining on our torch lit deck, overlooking the resort and beautiful Catskill scenery, or in the casual atmosphere of our main dinning room. Whether you're coming in off the river or just want to enjoy a night out, our late night service, and our chef's carefully prepared dinners are sure to satisfy your appetite.

The River Run Restaurant features the Trout Skellar as its lower level pub, with a cherry bar, stone floor, oak seating, pool table, juke box, and a field stone fireplace that creates a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Corporate Events/Meetings/Special Events

The River Run Restaurant is recently being expanded to almost double the size. This increased square footage was designed not only for those looking for a relaxing dining experience in our fabulous dining room, but also for those looking for that special place to host their events. The expansion has the capability of offering a total of 3600 square feet, or be split into two separate rooms that will give privacy for each event. From corporate meetings, wedding and baby showers, rehearsal dinners, Christmas and birthday parties to family reunions, the River Run Restaurant will make it one to remember.

Corporate events are easily accommodated. The main restaurant, both meeting rooms and the TroutSkellar are all available if needed. The new meeting rooms were designed with today's high tech needs in mind, with satellite internet connections, and audio visual needs ready for any type of meeting. In addition, for larger groups the entire resort may be reserved at a special rate. Call us for more details for what will be a totally unique business/activity meeting for your company.
