Orvis Endorsed Fly Fishing Schools at West Branch Angler

In keeping with the tradition of the oldest fly fishing school in America, West Branch Angler will be offering three 2 1/2 day fly fishing schools. These hands-on schools are a great introduction to the sport of fly fishing.


During the 2 1/2 day school our instructors will teach you:

  • Casting technique
  • Essential Knots
  • Choosing gear and tackle
  • Stream Entomology
  • Proper fly selection
  • Playing, landing and safely releasing fish

Scheduled School Dates:

  • July 9-11
  • August 20-22
  • September 17-19

All Schools will include:

  • Comprehensive instruction
  • New York fishing License
  • Daily lunches
  • Use of Orvis rod, reel, line and waders
  • Private access to the West Branch of the Delaware River before and after class.
  • 10% shop-discount on Orvis merchandise

The price of the 2 1/2 day school package:
$490.00
