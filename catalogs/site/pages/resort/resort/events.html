Thunderbirds To Perform At The Broome County Air Show
If you are looking for something different to do while you're staying with us, here's a local event that promises to be some great fun. This years Broome County Air Show is July 10 and 11, and it is the first time the show will be a two day event.

The USAF Thunderbirds fly Lockheed Martin F-16's that can reach speeds up to Fourteen-hundred miles an hour. Captain Dave Haworth from the Thunderbirds says, "It's a thrill, it's like going and seeing a rollercoaster ride being built right in front of them, because that's what they're going to see. Six demonstration planes, put on an air show like they've never seen before." This is the Thunderbirds only performance in New York State this summer.
 
A Great Loss!!

It is with profound sadness that we have to report that a true legend of the Delaware river system has recently passed away. Jim Charron, know by one and all as 'Jimmy' had guided and been a part of our system for longer than most of us have been fishing. He was a not only a very talented fly fisherman and guide for the WBA, but a most accomplished fly tyer as well. The quality and innovation of his comparadun patterns stood alone to those who knew of his talent. His knowledge of entomology, trout behavior, and of the system will be missed by all. He was a good friend and a true gentleman. . for those qualities he will be even more sorely missed.
