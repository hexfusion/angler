West Branch Angler Resort
 


“The Premier Outdoor Destination in the East”
The West Branch Angler Resort is nestled in the wooded foothills of the Catskills on the banks of the West Branch of the Delaware River, offering guests front door access to the best wild trout fishery in the East. Our full service resort offers the best of the best, with three miles of private prime fly fishing water, a pro staff of experienced river guides, world class fly shop, Orvis Endorsed upscale log cabin accommodations, sporting clays, fine dining, and a host of family oriented outdoor recreations second to none.

West Branch Angler Resort is conveniently located between Deposit and Hancock NY with quick access from RT17 and less than 3 hours from NYC. West Branch Angler & Resort has quickly grown into the Mecca for novice and experienced anglers because of its wonderful atmosphere, helpful experienced staff, and upscale riverside log cabin accommodations. The 70+ river miles of world class western class wild trout fishing including rivers such as the Beaverkill, Willowemoc, East, West and Main branches of the Delaware all within 30 miles.
West Branch Angler to carry Winston Fly Rods
West Branch Angler is constantly striving to bring its customers the best in Fly Fishing gear and accessories. In correlation with these standards we are proud to announce our business partnership with Winston Fly Rods. We feel that the Winston Fly Rod Company will fit perfectly with our novice to expert client base. As of the 1st of the year we will have the Winston rods in stock and ready for you to try out. September 1st: Bird Hunting Preserve is Now Open !


Upland Bird Hunting is now available at the West Branch Resort. We offer customized guide packages to fit any hunter’s needs. It does not matter if you are an experienced bird hunter with your own dog, or someone interested in entering a new field of an old sporting tradition, we can offer you a truly unique experience that you will not soon forget.

We have several trained bird dogs available for your adventure. These dogs are all pure bred and live for only one reason….. “to hunt.” Our dogs consist of English Setters, English Pointers and Black Labs and they will vigorously work to make sure that they find you all the pheasant, chukar, or grouse that you can handle.

The lots we are hunting are adjacent to the West Branch of the Delaware and consist of small plots of woods, rows of corn, and heavy concentrations of sorghum, proving excellent cover for birds and challenging, yet fun shots for all the members in your party.

At the end of your hunt, our fully trained guides will field dress your fresh kills and prepare them for your transportation home. Or if you decide to stay with us, they can provide excellent recipes for a BBQ that evening. This will surly be an event that you and your friends will enjoy and remember forever.

If you are interested in learning more about upland bird hunting, please call for more details at 800-201-2557.
