West Branch Angler Outdoor Adventures

Even though fly fishing is our primary function, hiking, biking and nature watching is something that all can enjoy at the West Branch Angler. Whether a single family group or a corporate meeting, the option of outdoor activities that does not include fly fishing can round out a trip to our resort. In order to better serve our guests, we now offer alternative outdoor activities that most everyone can participate in. West Branch Angler Resort can assist you by arranging hiking, mountain biking, canoeing and other non-fishing activities that offers some fun outdoor sports.

Perfect for corporate parties or small groups, the West Branch Angler can help open the doorway to the beauty of the Catskill Mountains. From hike-in cookouts to scenic canoe trips along the Delaware River we can arrange a wilderness exploration for you or your group. Call 1.800.201.2557 to make a reservation for your adventure.
 
Other Local Activities
GOLF 
  • Hancock Golf Course: Golf Course Rd., Hancock, NY - 9 holes, scheduled tee times.
  • Lake Lorain Golf Club: Rt. 370, Poyntelle, PA.
  • Scotts Oquaga Lake House Golf Course, Deposit, NY

PARKS/OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES

  • Oquaga Creek State: Deposit, NY - Basketball, hiking, biking, boating, fishing, fitness trail, horseshoes, picnic area, lake, winter ice skating, tobogganing, snowshoeing, sledding, cross country skiing.
  • Ross Park Zoo: Binghamton, NY - Established in 1875. Fifth oldest zoo in the country. 75 acres, picnic facilities, gift shop.
  • Delaware & Ulster Rail Ride - Rail ride along the Catskill Scenic Trail (Arkville to Highmont and Arkville to Roxbury).
  • Bear Spring State Park: Walton, NY - Horses, hiking, ponds, swimming, volleyball.

MUSEUMS AND HISTORY

  • Roberson Museum and Science Center: Binghamton, NY - Discovery Center, Kopernick Observatory, 19th & 20th Century Museum.
  • Equinuk Historical Society: Equinuk, PA - Calder House Museum, Shop & Hills Saw Mill.
  • Catskill Fly Fishing Museum: Roscoe, NY
  • Deposit Historical Society: Deposit, NY
  • Hancock Historical Society: Hancock, NY
  • Baseball Hall of Fame Museum: Cooperstown, NY
  • Farmer's Museum: Cooperstown, NY
