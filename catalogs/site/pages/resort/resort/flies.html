There are many marvelous creatures in this world.  None are any more beautiful or wonderful than the insects we call Mayflies and Caddisflies.  To see them at the rivers edge, or watch them dance in the air in a mating ritual, is to know that such a creation is alive for a purpose. As Trout fisherman we know and appreciate the purpose, for without them, fly fishing for trout and even the trout themselves might just cease to exist.

Attempting to accurately imitate such marvelous alien beings is never easy.  Many have tried with "generic" patterns.  Some have created a fly that is effective on one river, but is ineffective on another.  At the West Branch Angler many of the flies we sell are quality designed and crafted by local tiers in the Catskills.  They represent years of experience in finding just the right color, size, body and wing configuration, plus the excellent quality needed to fool the wary trout in the Delaware river system and beyond.

We asked the WBA Pro Staff Guides, who fish the system every day, to go through our huge inventory and pick the flies they have found most effective for them.  Their selections were excellent, so the flies you will find listed are those that have produced results time after time for their clients.
