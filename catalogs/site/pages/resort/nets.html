The Zephyr Composite Series

These are some of the nicest, lightest most fish friendly nets we have ever seen.  The Zephyr Composite Series of landing nets are 100% carbon fiber, molded nets.  Extremely light in weight, (they float like corks), but tough enough to withstand all the elements that you put up with when fishing, whether you are wading, float tubing, or in a boat.   Designed and tested on the blue ribbon waters of Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho,  Zephyrs are the landing nets you can count on time and time again.  These are some great looking and great performing nets! 

Zephyr Z1

A 100% carbon fiber personal landing net with an offset handle that reduces or eliminates the need to angle one's wrist when landing a fish.  The bow size and the net bag combine to accommodate fish up to 24".  Comes standard with the Snake River Sling, a lanyard that doubles as a carrier and a fish-friendly, NSB* flat-bottom 10" deep nylon net bag.

LZ-1

The LZ-1 is the smaller cousin to the Zephyr ASX.  The LZ-1 has the same offset handle as the Zephyr and also comes standard with the Snake River Sling.  Fish up to 18" will fit in this net that has a fish-friendly NSB* flat-bottom 10" deep nylon net bag.

*The NSB or "No Snag Bag", is made from nylon mesh that has been dipped in proprietary neoprene mixture that has been formulated to resist UV and ozone degradation.  The neoprene fills in the threads making it very difficult to snag hooks.  This one won't snag even multiple hook sets, and is the lightest most snag resistant bag on the market.  The neoprene also adds body to the mesh, allowing the net bad to remain open, forming a mini-aquarium for reviving fish.  After landing, unhook the fish let it swim inside the the net to revive.  Once the fish is ready, lower the frame and let it swim away.  The bag is made to soften with use, but will never loose its shape.  This truly is the most fish friendly net bag we have ever used at the West Branch Angler!
