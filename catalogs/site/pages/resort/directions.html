Directions

 

From New York City: Take the Major Degan North to the New York Throughway (I-87). Take I-87 north to Exit 16 (Harriman Exit). Pay toll and travel west on Route 17. Approximately 5 miles past Exit 87 (Hancock Exit) watch for turn off for Hale Eddy. Go into left turn lane, and turn left off Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Philadelphia, PA: Take the North East Turnpike Extension north to I-81. Take I-81 north to (Route 17). Take Route 17 east. Approximately 4 miles past Exit 84 (Deposit Exit), watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Albany, NY: Take I-88 west to Exit 9 (Sydney Exit). Take Route 8 until the road ends. Turn left at the T, taking the on ramp up onto Route 17 East. Take Route 17 East for approximately 4 miles, and watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Syracuse, NY: Take I-81 south to Route 17. Take Route 17/I-81 south to Route 17 east. Take Route 17 east. Take Route 17 East. Approximately 4 miles past Exit 84 (Deposit Exit), watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Buffalo, NY: Go east on I-90 to I-690. Take I-690 east to I-81. Go south on I-81 to State Route 17. Take State Route 17 East. Take Route 17 east. Approximately 4 miles past Exit 84 (Deposit Exit), watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Baltimore, MD: Go north on I-83 to Pennsylvania. Head northwest on I-83 to I-81. Go north on I-81 to New York. Go I-81 north to State Route 17. Take State Route 17 East. Approximately 4 miles past Exit 84 (Deposit Exit), watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.


From Boston, MA: Go south on I-93 to I-90. Take I-90 west to New York. Go northwest on I-90 to I-88. Go West on I-88 to Exit 9 (State Route 8). Take Route 8 until it ends. Turn left at the T, taking the on ramp up onto Route 17 East. Take Route 17 East for approximately 4 miles, and watch for the turn off for Hale Eddy. Turn right off of Route 17 and proceed across the Hale Eddy Bridge. Turn left just as you come off the bridge onto the dirt road (Faulkner Road). Take Faulkner Road 3/4 of a mile to West Branch Angler Resort.
