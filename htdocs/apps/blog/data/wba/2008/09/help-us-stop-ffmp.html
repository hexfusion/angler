Help Us Stop FFMP

By Sam Batschelet on September 23, 2008 7:12 PM | No Comments | No TrackBacks
"The Delaware River Basin Commission (DRBC) is scheduled to hold a vote on September 24th to amend the Water Code under which the commission operates in order to legitimize the interim Flexible Flow Management Program (FFMP) for the Upper Delaware River. Trout Unlimited urges you to visit our online action center today and tell the DRBC to postpone the vote on the Water Code until they have fully evaluated the performance of the interim FFMP and incorporated into the FFMP the changes required to ensure an ecologically healthy river system." -TU
