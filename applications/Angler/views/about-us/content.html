About us

At West Branch Angler we pride ourselves in bringing you the best equipment and fly fishing knowledge in the industry. At our core we are all fly fisherman our love and passion is what has built this company from it's humble roots to the premier fly fishing resort in the United States. Family owned and operated West Branch Angler & Resort sits on 2 miles of private water on the West Branch of the Delaware River. Our local technical wild trout population demand absolute perfection. Our quest to achieve this perfection means we try a lot of techniques and gear. We want to bring these experiences to you. "Fly Fishing The Delaware" - Since 1991
